CarCaine Community Guidelines

Effective Date: November 6, 2025

Our Commitment

CarCaine is a community for car enthusiasts to share their passion, showcase their builds, and connect with like-minded people. We are committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment for all users.

Core Values

Precision: We value accuracy, authenticity, and honest representation of your vehicles and builds.

Passion: We celebrate enthusiasm, creativity, and dedication to automotive culture.

Respect: We treat all community members with dignity and respect, regardless of background, beliefs, or preferences.

Safety: We prioritize the safety and well-being of our community members and the public.

What We Allow

CarCaine welcomes content that celebrates automotive culture, including:

  • High-quality photos and videos of vehicles
  • Build logs and modification documentation
  • Restoration and customization projects
  • DIY tutorials and maintenance guides
  • Car meet and event announcements
  • Community discussions and recommendations
  • Respectful debate about automotive topics
  • Support and encouragement for fellow enthusiasts

What We Don't Allow

1. Illegal Activity

We do not permit content that promotes, encourages, or facilitates illegal activity, including:

  • Illegal Racing: Street racing, drag racing on public roads, or any racing outside designated tracks
  • Reckless Driving: Dangerous driving, excessive speeding, or stunts that endanger others
  • Vehicle Violations: Illegal modifications, unregistered vehicles, or expired registrations
  • Other Crimes: Theft, fraud, drug use, or any other illegal activity

Enforcement: Violations may result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to law enforcement.

2. Harassment and Abuse

We do not permit content that harasses, bullies, or abuses others, including:

  • Personal attacks or insults
  • Threats of violence or harm
  • Doxxing or sharing private information
  • Coordinated harassment campaigns
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on protected characteristics

Enforcement: First violation results in a warning. Repeated violations result in account suspension or termination.

3. Explicit and Adult Content

We do not permit explicit, adult, or sexually explicit content, including:

  • Nudity or sexually explicit material
  • Pornographic or adult content
  • Sexually suggestive captions or comments
  • Content that sexualizes or objectifies people

Enforcement: Content is removed immediately. Repeated violations result in account suspension.

4. Misinformation and Spam

We do not permit false, misleading, or spam content, including:

  • False information about vehicles or modifications
  • Misleading claims about products or services
  • Spam or repetitive promotional content
  • Phishing or scam attempts
  • Impersonation or fake accounts

Enforcement: Content is removed. Repeated violations result in account suspension.

5. Intellectual Property Violations

We do not permit content that infringes on intellectual property rights, including:

  • Copyrighted photos or videos without permission
  • Trademarked content without authorization
  • Plagiarized content or ideas
  • Unauthorized use of others' builds or designs

Enforcement: Content is removed. Repeated violations result in account suspension.

6. Dangerous or Harmful Content

We do not permit content that could cause harm, including:

  • Instructions for illegal modifications
  • Dangerous driving techniques
  • Content that endangers public safety
  • Self-harm or suicide promotion
  • Animal abuse

Enforcement: Content is removed immediately. Accounts may be suspended or terminated.

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, please report it using the in-app reporting feature. When reporting, provide:

  • What happened: Description of the violation
  • When it happened: Approximate date and time
  • Evidence: Screenshots or links to the content
  • Context: Any additional information that helps us understand the issue

We review all reports and take appropriate action within 24-48 hours.

Appeals

If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you can appeal within 30 days by contacting us at appeals@carcaine.app. We will review your appeal and respond within 7 days.

Moderator Conduct

Our moderation team is trained to:

  • Apply these guidelines fairly and consistently
  • Respect user privacy and dignity
  • Explain enforcement decisions clearly
  • Consider context and intent
  • Provide appeals opportunities

Moderators are bound by confidentiality and will not share user information.

Community Responsibility

All users have a role in maintaining a safe and respectful community:

  • Follow these guidelines in your posts and comments
  • Treat other users with respect
  • Report violations you encounter
  • Don't engage with or amplify harmful content
  • Support fellow community members
  • Provide constructive feedback

Special Topics

Car Meets and Events

Event organizers are responsible for:

  • Ensuring events comply with local laws
  • Providing clear safety information
  • Monitoring attendee conduct
  • Reporting illegal activity to authorities
  • Canceling events that become unsafe

Attendees are responsible for:

  • Following all local laws and regulations
  • Respecting event rules and safety guidelines
  • Treating other attendees with respect
  • Not engaging in illegal racing or dangerous driving

Build Documentation

When documenting builds, we encourage:

  • Accurate descriptions of modifications
  • Clear documentation of parts and costs
  • Before/after photos
  • Timeline of work
  • Lessons learned and recommendations

We discourage:

  • Misleading claims about performance
  • Undocumented or illegal modifications
  • Plagiarism of others' builds
  • Dangerous modification techniques

Contact Us

For questions about these guidelines or to report violations, contact us at:

CarCaine Community Team

In-App: Use the report feature

Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these guidelines to address new issues or improve clarity. We will notify users of significant changes and provide a grace period for compliance.
